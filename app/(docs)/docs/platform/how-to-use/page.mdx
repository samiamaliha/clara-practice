#### How to Use Nerzo Website

## Prerequisites

To use our platform, you need an **EVM-compatible wallet**. Examples:

- **MetaMask**
- **Trust Wallet**
- **WalletConnect**

## Connecting Your Wallet

1. **Open Nerzo**: Navigate to our website.
2. **Connect Wallet**: Click on the "Connect Wallet" button located at the top left corner of the page.
3. **Select Your Wallet**: Choose your EVM-compatible wallet from the options provided and follow the prompts to connect.

## Minting NFTs Without an EVM Wallet

If you don't have an EVM wallet, you can still use our platform and mint NFTs for free. Here's how:

1. **Email & Socials Wallet**: During the connection process on our website, select the option to use your email & Socials wallet.
2. **Gas-Free Minting**: With an email wallet, you can mint NFTs without needing gas.

> **Note**: This feature is only supported on **Superchain**.

## Additional Tips

- Ensure your wallet is properly funded and connected to avoid transaction issues.
- Familiarize yourself with the platform's interface to make the most of your NFT experience.
