This multichain approach not only broadens NERZO's market reach but also fortifies its platform against potential risks associated with relying on a single blockchain infrastructure.

## Phase 4: Market Expansion and Sustainability (September-20-2024)

### Claim Main Token:
Facilitate a seamless process for users to claim their main NERZO tokens, ensuring that all participants in the initial distribution have access to their tokens on both centralized and decentralized exchanges.

### Convert Test Token to Main Token:
Provide a mechanism for users who participated in any testnet or beta phases to convert their test tokens to main NERZO tokens, ensuring continuity of ownership and utility within the NERZO ecosystem. Presales and airdropped NRZ tokens are given as test tokens. An equal number of main tokens will be claimable before listing during this phase of our roadmap.

### Community Giveaway:
Organize community-focused events or campaigns to distribute NERZO tokens to a wider audience, fostering greater participation and engagement within the NERZO community while also promoting the token's utility and value.

500 million tokens are reserved for the community. These tokens are set to be completely free and will only be available to individuals who have completed all required tasks set out by NERZO. The community tokens will become available shortly before the token listing, typically around 1 to 2 days prior.

### Token Listing:
NERZO plans to list its token on both centralized exchanges (CEX) and decentralized exchanges (DEX) to increase its accessibility and liquidity on **3rd October, 2024**. The dual listing approach ensures that NERZO can cater to different preferences within the crypto community, whether users seek the security of CEXs or the independence of DEXs. It also significantly enhances the visibility of NERZO tokens within the broader cryptocurrency market, potentially increasing their value and the overall adoption of the NERZO platform.
